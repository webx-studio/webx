Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with WebX

How long does a typical project take?

Timelines vary based on project complexity. A standard website takes 6-8 weeks, while custom web applications can take 8-16 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during our discovery phase.

Do you offer ongoing maintenance?

Yes! We offer monthly maintenance packages starting at $500/month. This includes security updates, performance monitoring, backups, and technical support.

What's your payment structure?

We require a 50% deposit to begin work, 25% upon design approval, and the final 25% upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

Can you work with existing designs?

Absolutely! We can develop from your existing designs or create new ones. We work with Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, and other design tools.

Do you provide hosting services?

We partner with premium hosting providers and can manage hosting for you, or we can deploy to your preferred hosting service. We recommend and optimize for performance-focused hosting.

What technologies do you use?

We use modern technologies including React, Vue.js, Next.js, Node.js, Python, and various databases. We choose the best stack for each project's specific needs.
